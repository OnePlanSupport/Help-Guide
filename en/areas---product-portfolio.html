Areas - Product Portfolio

What is the Product Portfolio?

OnePlan's Product Portfolio area presents a holistic, centralized view and management of all product development initiatives within an organization. It provides an overview of product projects and epic statuses, budgets, and benefits, enhancing project visibility, control over resources, and facilitating strategic decision-making. Why Would You Want to Add It?

Adding the Product Portfolio to your OnePlan group gives your organization a centralized hub to monitor and manage all its product development activities. Not only does it streamline your workflow by having everything in one place, but it also makes decision-making more informed and efficient. It is about optimizing your processes, saving time, and driving your business success.

Example Use Case

For example, consider you are supervising a product team creating multiple software applications. OnePlan's Product Portfolio lets you review each app's progress, budget, and expected benefits. It also lets you quickly respond when product development projects go off course or near budget limits, helping you make more informed decisions and enhance efficiency in your product development.
