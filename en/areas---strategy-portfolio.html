Areas - Strategy Portfolio

What is the Strategy Portfolio?

OnePlan's Strategy Portfolio is designed as a practical and efficient solution to aid organizations in their strategic planning, execution, and evaluation processes. It encourages alignment within an organization, offering features to establish shared goals, monitor progress, manage budgets, and predict future benefits. It can be adapted for use across various departments including IT, Marketing, and Product Development, contributing to a comprehensive approach to strategic management.

Why Would You Want to Add It?

Adding the Strategy Portfolio to your OnePlan Group allows for a unified view of all strategic initiatives, enhancing decision-making with real-time data. It fosters better resource allocation, budget control, and facilitates tracking of progress and anticipated benefits across different departments, resulting in improved strategic alignment and execution.

Example Use Case

For instance, a company launching a new product line uses the OnePlan Strategy Portfolio to align team expectations, objectives, and budgets. The OnePlan Strategy Portfolio would help monitor development milestones, adjust to budget changes in real time, and project potential market benefits, ensuring compliance with their overall business strategy and facilitating effective communication across the project's lifecycle.
