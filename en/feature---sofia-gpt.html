Feature - Sofia GPT

What is Sofia GPT?

OnePlan's AI virtual assistant, Sofia, is designed to help you optimize the way you use OnePlan. By seamlessly integrating with OnePlan, Sofia processes and interprets large amounts of data to provide vital insights and propose actionable improvements tailored to your specific projects and processes. Sofia empowers users to make more well-informed and confident project decisions.

Why Would You Want to Add It?

As day-to-day responsibilities become more complex, having a tool like Sofia to help you navigate through complex project data can be a game-changer.

Sofia serves as your personalized virtual assistant, going beyond surface level analysis to offer deep dives into your projects. Sofia scans your organization's OnePlan data to understand your operations better, helping to improve efficacy and optimize performance across all areas within the OnePlan platform.

Example Use Case

For instance, if you want to understand trends within your Project Portfolio, you can simply ask, 'Could you examine the trends and provide a summary of the current state of my portfolio?' Sofia will comprehensively analyze all the data across your Project Portfolio, summarizing the status of each portfolio, whether at risk, on track, or off track, and clarify the reasons.
