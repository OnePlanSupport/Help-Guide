Feature - Teams

What is the Teams Feature in OnePlan?

OnePlan offers a Complete Project Portfolio Management solution for Microsoft Teams that enables project teams to stay connected and organized. With the OnePlan and Teams, users can define strategy, set priorities, capture requests, and execute projects all within one interface - Microsoft Teams.

Why Would You Want to Add It?

OnePlan and Teams helps you foster collaboration within your project teams and organization. You can create Teams sites for each project, enabling ongoing discussion threads, document management and direct access to tasks, issues, risks, and other key project information. Work and information are centralized in Teams, so users don’t have to go to several different locations to do work, update status, or find the information they are looking for. The success of your deliverables relies on strong communication and visibility into your project assets. OnePlan and Teams enables you to easily share information with your stakeholders in a single location and ensure teams are progressing towards common goals. Real-time visibility into project performance keeps teams aligned and stakeholders in the know.

Example Use Case

An example use case for using OnePlan and Microsoft Teams together could be for a project management team that wants to integrate their planning and communication. For instance, a project team is distributed across various locations and uses Microsoft Teams for daily communication and collaboration. With OnePlan's integration, the team can directly access their project plans, tasks, and timelines within the Teams interface. During team meetings on Teams, members can quickly pull up their OnePlan project dashboard, share their screen, and discuss project progress, task assignments, and upcoming deadlines without having to switch between applications. This integration would also allow team members to receive notifications within Microsoft Teams when changes are made to the project plan in OnePlan, ensuring everyone is kept updated.
