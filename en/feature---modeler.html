Feature - Modeler

What is the Modeler?

The OnePlan Modeler empowers users by providing a data-driven platform for simulating business scenarios, analyzing outcomes, and driving strategic decisions. It emphasizes proactive risk management, effective resource allocation, and maximization of business value, making it an invaluable tool in optimizing financial plans across portfolios, managing resources, and strategic business planning.

Why Would You Want to Add It?

Adding the Modeler to your OnePlan group allows for strategic, data-driven decision making and effective resource management. It aids in proactive risk reduction and financial planning, while enhancing project management and fostering better group collaboration. It is an essential tool for maximizing business value and optimizing overall strategy.

Example Use Case

For example, if your organization plans to launch multiple projects, then using the Modeler can help simulate various scenarios with different resource allocations and timelines. This helps identify the optimal mix of projects that delivers the maximum value while staying within budget and resource constraints.
