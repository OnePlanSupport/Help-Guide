My Capabilities

Business capability modeling is a crucial tool in enterprise architecture (EA). It helps organizations understand what they do (capabilities) rather than how they do it (processes). This distinction allows for a clear representation of organizational strengths and priorities, guiding confident decision-making and strategic planning.

My Capabilities Portfolio

The OnePlan My Capabilities area helps foster a capability-centric approach to EA that not only enhances operational efficiency but also strategically positions organizations to navigate future challenges and opportunities. This view offers an overview of business capabilities by State, Strategic Alignment, and Risk Reduction.

Each Capability comes with an exhaustive detail page, where you can manage the stages of the capability, and view associated Value Streams, Applications, and Products that support the Capability.
