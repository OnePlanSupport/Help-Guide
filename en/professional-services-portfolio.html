Professional Services Portfolio

What is the Professional Services Portfolio?

The OnePlan Professional Services portfolio is designed to help organizations plan and run their professional services more effectively. Services may include project portfolio management, resource management, strategic planning, and more. It helps businesses manage their plans, resources, financials, and business intelligence in one place, thereby improving transparency, productivity, and efficiency within the organization.

Example Use Case

Imagine a consulting firm handling many customers, projects, and finances all at once. Using OnePlan's Professional Services portfolio could transform their work by offering a one-stop solution for managing projects, resources, and finances. This can make their work more efficient, use resources better, and provide deeper financial insights. As a result, their service to clients becomes smoother and their professional reputation gets a nice boost.
