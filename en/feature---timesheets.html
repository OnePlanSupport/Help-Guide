Feature - Timesheets

What are Timesheets in OnePlan?

OnePlan’s Timesheet allows you to track time on all work that you are assigned to, as well as non-work items like vacation or holidays. Time tracking is crucial for billing and understanding one's work patterns. With OnePlan's Timesheet module, capturing time is made simple and efficient.

Why Would You Want to Add It?

OnePlan Timesheets make time tracking simple as you can conveniently import your time data from the Resource Plan or previous periods. This minimizes manual effort and potential errors. OnePlan also has a mobile app that team members can use to manage their Timesheets on the go. With OnePlan Timesheets, not only can you accurately capture your time, but you can also derive insights, ensure accurate billing, and optimize your work patterns for better productivity.

Example Use Case

An example use case for using Timesheets in OnePlan could be a project manager who wants to track the hours that their team members are spending on a particular project. They can use the OnePlan Timesheets to record the start time, end time, and total hours spent on each task by every team member. This allows the project manager to see at a glance who is spending too much or too little time on a task, enabling them to adjust workload distribution, identify potential bottlenecks, and determine if the project is on track according to the estimated timelines.
